
# Restaurant Market & Performance Analysis

## Project Overview

This project analyses restaurant market data to uncover insights into cuisine trends, pricing strategy, customer engagement, geographic distribution, and service offerings.

The objective is to transform raw restaurant data into actionable business intelligence that can support strategic decisions such as expansion planning, pricing optimisation, and service improvement.

---

## Business Problem

Restaurant operators and food delivery platforms need to understand:

* Which cuisines dominate the market?
* Which cities have the highest restaurant concentration?
* Do higher prices translate to better ratings?
* Does offering online delivery improve performance?
* What drives customer engagement (votes and reviews)?

This analysis answers these questions using data-driven methods.

---

## Dataset Description

The dataset includes restaurant-level information such as:

* Restaurant Name
* City
* Cuisines
* Price Range
* Aggregate Rating
* Votes
* Online Delivery Availability
* Table Booking Availability
* Latitude & Longitude
* Customer Reviews

The dataset was cleaned and transformed before analysis to ensure consistency and accuracy.

---

## Data Cleaning & Preparation

The following preprocessing steps were performed:

* Removed duplicate records
* Handled missing values
* Standardized cuisine naming conventions
* Cleaned inconsistent city labels
* Converted price range into categorical format
* Verified rating and vote numerical integrity
* Processed review text for keyword analysis

These steps ensured reliable analytical outputs.

---

## Exploratory Data Analysis

### Cuisine Trends

* Identified the top three most common cuisines.
* Calculated percentage market share of each cuisine.
* Analyzed popular cuisine combinations.

Key Insight: Certain cuisine categories dominate market share, indicating strong consumer preference concentration.

---

### City Performance Analysis

* Identified the city with the highest restaurant density.
* Calculated average rating per city.
* Determined the highest-rated city.

Key Insight: High restaurant density does not necessarily correlate with higher average ratings.

---

### Price Range Distribution

* Visualized distribution of restaurants across pricing tiers.
* Calculated percentage representation of each price category.

Key Insight: Mid-range pricing categories contain the highest market concentration.

---

### Online Delivery Impact

* Calculated percentage of restaurants offering online delivery.
* Compared average ratings between restaurants with and without delivery.

Key Insight: Restaurants offering delivery services show competitive rating performance and broader engagement.

---

### Ratings & Customer Engagement

* Analyzed aggregate rating distribution.
* Identified the most common rating band.
* Calculated average votes per restaurant.
* Explored correlation between votes and ratings.

Key Insight: Restaurants with higher vote counts tend to maintain stable, higher ratings, indicating visibility and trust effects.

## Key Business Recommendations
